About Me

Hello, I am Matthew Williams,I am from Texas with two Masters' Degrees and a passion for traveling the world. I have pursued two master's degrees, one from Texas A&M Texarkana where I studied American History and another from the University of Haifa in Israel where I studied Israel Studies. Over the years, I have lived in various places, including Israel, Washington D.C., China, and currently living and working in Korea since 2020.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my journey and interests!

Education
